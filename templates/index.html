About

TeenSense is a dual-module emotional well-being support system built to strengthen understanding between teenagers and parents. It offers teens a safe space to express their feelings through journaling while interactive flashcards help them recognize emotions and learn healthy coping strategies.

  • Safe space for teens to express thoughts and emotions through journaling
  • AI-powered emotional analysis to detect patterns and mood trends
  • Interactive flashcards that improve emotional awareness and coping skills
  • Timely alerts for parents when concerning emotional patterns are detected
  • Guided reminder flashcards that encourage supportive parent responses
