About

TeenSense is an AI-assisted academic well-being support system designed to address the growing challenges teenagers face in managing academic pressure and emotional health. Adolescence is a critical phase marked by increasing academic demands, including examinations, assignments, performance expectations, and future career concerns. During this time, communication between teenagers and parents often becomes limited, making it difficult for parents to fully understand the emotional impact of academic life.TeenSense bridges this gap by providing a structured and supportive digital environment that enables continuous yet non-intrusive interaction between parents and teenagers. The platform encourages students to reflect on their daily academic experiences-such as classroom learning, homework, assessments, and study-related challenges-through guided journaling and interactive flashcards.At its core, TeenSense incorporates an AI-driven emotion detection system that analyzes textual inputs to identify emotional states such as stress, anxiety, motivation, and disengagement. By performing both daily analysis and long-term trend tracking, the system detects patterns related to academic workload and performance pressure, helping identify early signs of burnout or declining motivation.To maintain a balanced approach, TeenSense emphasizes non-intrusive monitoring by providing summarized insights and gentle alerts to parents only when significant emotional patterns are observed. This ensures timely support while respecting the teenager’s independence and privacy.The platform also includes a Flashcard Module for structured communication and a Reminder Module for managing academic schedules such as exams, deadlines, and important events, promoting better organization and consistent academic engagement.By combining artificial intelligence with empathetic design, TeenSense fosters healthier study habits, emotional resilience, and stronger parent-teen relationships, supporting both academic success and overall well-being.
